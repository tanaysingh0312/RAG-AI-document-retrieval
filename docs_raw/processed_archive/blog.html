What is the Color Rendering Index?

The color rendering index (CRI), quantitatively measures the ability of the light source to precisely reproduce the colors of the object on which it illuminates. For any application where color appearance is important, considering CRI is critical.

Does CRI relate to color temperature?

While Purchasing LED Light, you need to determine the color temperature of the LED Light(Weather it is cool or warm Light). This is measured in kelvin and ranges between 2700K(warm) to 6500K(Cool). When you want to determine the quality of Light source, you need to look at CRI of Light source, not at the color temperature of the light source. There is no correlation between them, they are independent. Don’t assume 5000K will automatically have a better CRI than 2700K because it is a whiter light.

Points to Remember Before Installing LED Lights

Now a day, throughout the world, people are switching from their traditional lights to LED lights.LED lights are quite versatile and can be used almost anywhere and everywhere, but people correlate them with discotheques, pubs, and hotels. Their application could be as vast as your creative imagination. If you are thinking to try this amazing, efficient and environment-friendly LED Lights, then here are some points you should consider before installing it.

What Kind of Lighting Affect You Want To Create?

Once installed, the result may not be exactly the same as what you’ve imagined. So, it is very important to be clear about the position where you want to install your lights.
